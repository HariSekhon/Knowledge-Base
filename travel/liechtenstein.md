## Summary

[Liechtenstein](Liechtenstein) is so small it doesn't even make sense to be a _"country"_.

It sits between Austria and Switzerland and German is the spoken language here.

I don't know what the point of this country is other
than to tick it off the list if you're in the area as it's only a 1 hour drive from [Zurich](switzerland.md#zurich).

One nice thing is that you can see the Swiss Alps from these villages posing as cities,
but the view and everything else is better in Switzerland.

Unless you are using this as a tax haven to save millions I just don't see the point of it,
and even then I wouldn't want to live here,
there are far better places like [Dubai](uae.md#dubai) to avoid the scam of high tax democracy.

## Driving in Liechtenstein

Driving is absolutely essential in Liechtenstein.

You can drive from one _"city"_ to the next in about 2-5 minutes.

You don't need any vignette like many other countries.

## Vaduz

[Vaduz](https://maps.app.goo.gl/kMMi6abxEisc7E9h8) is technically the _"capital"_ of Liechtenstein but seriously it's
only the size of a village in most of Europe.
You can walk it's main street end to end in about 5-8 minutes.
