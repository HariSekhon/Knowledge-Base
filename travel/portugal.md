## Faro

Small town, you can walk around it in around half an hour.

- [Gula Foodlab](https://maps.app.goo.gl/p1y6xziy4gjNau6t5) - good quality ice cream - the coconut lime and
  gianduia (chocolate and hazelnut) are my two favourite flavours here, although their pistacchio is also very good.
