## Montevideo

### Best Areas to Stay in Montevideo

- [Pocitos](https://maps.app.goo.gl/V1LFxCFLaR5xLNjU9) - popular beach neighborhood with restaurants, nightlife,
  and a lively promenade
- [Ciudad Vieja](https://maps.app.goo.gl/NUMirNp5PsULMq9x5) - historic old town with museums, colonial architecture,
  and nightlife
- [Punta Carretas](https://maps.app.goo.gl/EYMFFE4LmyPFv9Kp9) - upmarket coastal area with parks, shopping,
  and a relaxed atmosphere
- [Parque Rodó](https://maps.app.goo.gl/QbuGf2m1gvmc7N23A) - youthful area near the park and beach with cafes and bars
- [Cordón](https://maps.app.goo.gl/g4GHKG96p192AEDn9) - downtown, cheaper neighborhood with student vibe
  and good local food
- [Carrasco](https://maps.app.goo.gl/czYnXuMR68mhyQYR7) - Quiet upscale district with beaches
  and elegant residential streets
