## Visa

British citizens require no visa at all and could even live in Ireland.

Although they have similar problems to the UK with mass immigration and high housing costs.

Restore Ireland.

## Dublin

Dublin is a very multicultural city with many foreigners, similar to London.

As such it's only mildly Irish with a few pubs and touristy restaurants, but otherwise it could be anywhere.

Even when you go to other countries you find the Irish pubs, making the difference between western cities
relatively small.

Dublin is also expensive to dine out in, with mains typically cost around €30 each.

### Dublin Airport

Dublin Airport has free wifi with simple one button connect captcha portal.

#### Travel from Dublin Airport

The Dublin Express coach has stops immediately outside the airport for €10 each way -
cheaper than spending €25-30 on a taxi.

It leaves every 20 minutes and takes 25-30 minutes to Dublin centre.

You can buy the ticket online using the QR code at the coach stop.
