# Cuba

## Summary

I cancelled my plans to visit Cuba in 2026 due to infrastructure, issues with fuel, flights and visiting Cuba causes you
to lose eligibility for a US ESTA forever!

You would then have to apply for a formal US visa (expensive) for any future trips to the US or even just for a layover
flight!

One of my more adventurous travelling Brit friends has had his US ESTA rejected due to visiting a sanctioned country.
