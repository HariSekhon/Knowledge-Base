# Haiti

## Summary

Haiti was apparently at one time the richest colony in the Caribbean before being handed over to the local Africans.

It has since deteriorated in a similar fashion to other African countries like South Africa and Zimbabwe.

While spending time in the [Dominican Republic](dominican-republic.md) (a country I rank quite low
after spending nearly 2 weeks there), I was getting mean mugged passing by local africans there while walking with a
fellow traveller who was a bit more familiar with the region made the observation that some of the less reputable local
looking people are likely immigrants from the neighbouring Haiti, a country which is notoriously higher crime.

I'd already been warned about Haiti being high crime in
a discussion with a local native taxi driver in [Curacao](curacao.md) previous to this.

Given that it is a 3rd world country, and I'd just had enough of 3rd world countries devoid of IQ, looks, genetics
and quality civilization at this point on my world tour, I decided to skip this one and hasten by return to Europe instead.

I have, since returning to Europe, seen a YouTube video criticising Haiti and comparing it to other African countries
with similar problems in both development and maintenance of an inherited country.

I have no plans to visit Haiti in the foreseeable future for any reason.
