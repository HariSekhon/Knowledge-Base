# Honduras

## Summary

Skipped due to visa bureaucracy - they don't just have visa-on-arrival like a normal country due in retaliation to a
visa dispute with the UK having enforced stricter visa controls on them, which does not imply good things.

I couldn't get a visa within 2 days, it takes 2 days just to get the form and then 3 weeks to get the visa:
