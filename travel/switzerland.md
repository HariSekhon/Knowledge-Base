# Switzerland

## Driving in Switzerland

You need an e-vignette to drive on Swiss motorways.

Unfortunately they only sell it per calendar year and not for shorter durations.

Buy it here to avoid fines:

<https://via.admin.ch/shop/dashboard>

It costs 40CHF (£38) as of 2026. Pay in CHF to avoid a higher conversion upcharge to £40.

Speeding cameras are grey and easy to not see until it's too late and you're in snapshot distance,
so stick to the speed limits, and watch out for picking up speed on downhill alps motorways where cameras may be waiting
to catch you out!
