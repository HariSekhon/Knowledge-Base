## Maastricht

[Maastricht](https://maps.app.goo.gl/QJwg8E4mmQ2NJyEs5) - cobbled streets, no parking and low birthrates - welcome
to Europe.

Seriously though, Maastricht is pretty nice for a visit - you can walk around the centre in about an hour.

### Parking in Maastricht

The streets are all residents permit parking only, so you can't park almost anywhere.

- [Q-Park Frontenpark](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cJGdscnhYTfq9iE18) - €4.50 for just over an hour
- [P+R Station Maastricht Noord](https://maps.app.goo.gl/LzU2Vm3k8AocQSGq6) - free parking but further out you'd need to
  take buses into the city
